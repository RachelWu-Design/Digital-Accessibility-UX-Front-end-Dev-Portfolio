What Makes This Different?

Traditional accessibility focuses on compliance. Trauma-informed accessibility recognizes that digital barriers often mirror systemic exclusion and can create additional challenges for users who've faced discrimination.

My approach centers psychological safety, cultural responsiveness, and community voice in every design decision.
